Alfa Romeo

Creative suite produced for agency client, Alfa Romeo. The purpose of the material was to support all hospitality activations such as the annual Monterey Car Week, New York City Wine & Food Festival, Amelia Island Concours, LA Film Festival, and more.

This included designing the layout of the activation, graphics to support all test-drives, and pitching unique activities for guests on-site.
