Thursdays at the Museum



The Institution's Public Programming department requested a rebrand of their Thursdays at the Museum program. Pulling from the museum's architectural roots and the founders' mission, this acted as the foundation for the identity. The brand codes were applied to all video production, print, and digital materials and used by the internal team.
