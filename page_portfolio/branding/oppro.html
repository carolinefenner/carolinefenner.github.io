OPPRO

Logo concept for Opportunity Roasters (OPPRO for short), a student-run coffee shop. This will be located in the non-profit organization called Life Remodeled located in Detroit, Michigan USA. The purpose of the coffee shop is to empower local high school students, who are participating in the OPPRO program, to learn how to run, manage, and market the new neighborhood coffee shop.

Role: Senior Art Director
